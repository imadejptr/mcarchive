SLOPE [HTML5]

November 13th, 2023 - November 30th, 2023

One of the most popular web games of all time, Slope is a game that is played by many around the world. So it was a perfect choice for a moony challenge. The goal of this challenge was to get the highest score by rolling down a green and black hill, while trying to avoid the red block obstacles. This was the largest competition that was ever held on imadejptr, with 4 times the amount of contestants compared to the last competition.
